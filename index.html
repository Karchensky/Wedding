Getting There

Castello del Trebbio is located approximately 20 kilometers northeast of Florence, in the heart of the Chianti wine region. The journey from the airports is scenic and straightforward.

From Florence Airport (FLR)

Recommended | 45 minutes

Rental Car

Most flexible option. Major rental agencies at the airport. GPS recommended for navigating Tuscan roads.

Private Transfer

Pre-arranged transfers available. We can provide vetted driver recommendations upon request.

Taxi

Available at the airport. Approximate cost: 60-80 EUR to the castle.

From Rome Fiumicino (FCO)

3-3.5 hours

High-Speed Train + Transfer

Take the Frecciarossa or Italo train from Roma Termini to Firenze Santa Maria Novella (1.5 hours). Then taxi or rental car to the castle.

Rental Car

Scenic drive through Umbria and Tuscany. Approximately 280 km via A1 Autostrada.

From Buffalo / Western NY

Toronto Pearson (YYZ)

Just 1.5 hours from Buffalo. Air Canada offers direct flights to Rome (FCO). Often better prices and schedules than US options.

Buffalo to NYC Connection

Fly Buffalo to JFK/Newark, then connect to Rome or Florence. More options but longer travel day.

Consider a Stopover

Breaking up the journey with a night in Toronto, NYC, or a European hub can make the trip more comfortable.

Passport Note

Flying from Toronto requires a valid passport for both Canada and Italy. Ensure yours is valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates.

Extend Your Stay

Make the most of your journey to Italy

City

Florence

The Renaissance capital. Home to the Uffizi, Duomo, Ponte Vecchio, and world-class cuisine. Just 30 minutes from the castle.

  • Uffizi Gallery
  • Accademia (David)
  • San Lorenzo Market
  • Piazzale Michelangelo sunset
Wine

Chianti Region

The castle sits in the heart of Chianti. Rolling hills, medieval villages, and legendary wineries await.

  • Wine tasting tours
  • Greve in Chianti
  • Radda in Chianti
  • Olive oil tastings
Day Trips

Tuscan Towns

Iconic hilltop towns within easy driving distance. Each with unique character and history.

  • Siena (1 hour)
  • San Gimignano (1.5 hours)
  • Lucca (1.5 hours)
  • Pisa (1.5 hours)
Experience

  • Market tours

  • Swimming spots
  • Day trip or overnight
